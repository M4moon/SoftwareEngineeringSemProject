Cajun Shrimp Pasta Alfredo

{% include 'navbar.html' %}

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Cajun seasoning
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook shrimp with Cajun seasoning and butter.
  3. Add heavy cream and Parmesan, stir until the sauce thickens.
  4. Toss in cooked pasta and garnish with chopped parsley.

Cook Time:

25 minutes

  4. پکی ہوئی پاستا ڈالیں اور چھوٹی کٹی ہوئی پارسلی سے چھڑکیں۔
